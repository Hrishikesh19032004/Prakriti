DISCOVER YOUR PRAKRITI

UNVIELING YOUR UNIQUE DOSHA BLUEPRINT

Prakriti is a fundamental concept in Ayurveda,
An ancient system of medicine and wellness that
Originated in India. It refers to an individual's unique
Constitution or natural state of being. According to
Ayurveda, every person is born with a specific
Prakriti, which is determined by a combination of the
Three doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha. These doshas
Represent different combinations of the five elements
(earth, water, fire, air, and ether) and govern various
Physiological and psychological aspects of
An individual's health and well-being.

Learn More

Vata: Vata is associated with the elements of air and ether.
People with a predominant Vata constitution tend to be
creative, energetic, and enthusiastic when balanced.

Pitta: Pitta is linked to the elements of fire and water.
Those with a dominant Pitta constitution are often
passionate, organized, and goal-oriented when in balance.

Kapha: Kapha is connected to the elements of earth and
water. Individuals with a predominant Kapha
constitution are typically stable, calm, and nurturing
when balanced.
